Command Description Example
.box <box number> Check your box. Default box is 1. Check a trainer's box by tagging them or using their ID after the command .box
.boxprices Check the box prices .boxprices
.buybox Buy box space. Each box comes with 30 available spots for you to store your Pokémon .buybox
.boxpk <box number> <Pokémon position> Check a Pokémon's information from your box. Skip number 1 if you wish to view your first box. First box is the deault one .boxpk 1 a1
.boxswap <number> <Pokémon> Store Pokémon into your box. Default box number is 1. It is not necessary to add it .boxswap 1 Bulbasaur
.boxswap <box number> <Pokémon position> Take Pokémon from your box. Default box number is 1. It is not necessary to add it .boxswap 1 a1
.boxswap <box number> <Pokémon> <Pokémon position> Sent Pokémon to your box and take out at the same time. For Pokémon in your team you must type their name. For Pokémon in your box you must type their box position .boxswap 1 Bulbasaur Squirtle Charmander a1 a2 a3
.take boxpk <box number> <box position> Take an item from a Pokémon in your box .take boxpk 1 a1
.boxrelease <box number> <box position> Release a Pokémon from your box .boxrelease 1 a1
.boxmove <box number> <box number> <Pokémon position> Swap Pokémon between boxes. First the number of the box you wish to take Pokémon from, then the number of the box you wish to move the Pokémon to. Then the Pokémon positions you wish to move from one box to another. You can move multiple Pokémon at the same time .boxmove 1 2 a1 a2 a3
.daycare Check the Day Care .daycare
.raise <Pokémon>, <Pokémon> Leave two Pokémon at the Day Care .raise Charizard, Ditto
.get eggs Get the eggs from the Day Care to your team .get eggs
.get <Pokémon> Get a Pokémon from the Day Care .get Charizard
Command Description Example
.items <number> Check the items' list. The number indicates the page you are viewing .items 1
.buy <item> Buy an item once or multiple times. If you are buying it once you don't need to add a number .buy Leftovers 5
.sell <item> Sell an item once or multiple times. If you are selling it once you don't need to add a number .sell Leftovers 5
.give <Pokémon> <item> Give an item to a Pokémon .give Bulbasaur Leftovers
.take <Pokémon> Take an item from a Pokémon .take Bulbasaur
.take team Take all the items from your team .take team
.pokedex <number> Check all Pokémon sorted from A-Z. Default page is 1. Change the number to view a different page .pokedex 1
.pokedex <Pokémon> Check various information about a Pokémon .pokedex Bulbasaur
.pokedex Caught/ Captured Check how many Pokémon you have captured .pokedex Caught
.pokedex Remain/ Remaining Check how many Pokémon remain to be captured .pokedex Remain
.pokedex <type> Check Pokémon listed by type .pokedex Grass
.moves <Pokémon> <type> Check a Pokémon's moves sorted by type .moves Bulbasaur Grass
.moves <Pokémon> Physical/ Special/ Status/ Level/ Damage/ Accuracy Check a Pokémon's moves sorted by one of the options .moves Bulbasaur Physical
.mvinfo <move> Check some information regarding a move .mvinfo Phychic
.ability <ability> Check some information regarding an ability .ability Levitate
.item <item> Check some information regarding an item .item Leftovers
.natures Check the effects each nature has .natures
.type <type> Check the weaknesses resistances and immunities each Pokémon type has .type Fire
